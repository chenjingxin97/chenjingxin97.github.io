The code for spectral data processing.

  Astronomy is a long-established observational science. With the development of science and technology, observation equipment has been continuously upgraded, and human understanding of the universe has gradually expanded from the near to far.
  Advanced observing equipment enables us to look deeper into the universe and at the same time bring about explosive growth in astronomical data. Today, artificial intelligence (AI) technology can assist astronomers in the processing and analysis of massive astronomical data, thereby discovering new celestial bodies and physical laws.
  This project is to use the Python programming language to analyze and classify astronomical data.
